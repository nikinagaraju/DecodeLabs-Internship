📋 Extracted Text (Tesseract OCR Engine)
How It Works

Uses Tesseract.js — a port of Google's OCR engine. It scans the image pixel by pixel, detects character shapes, and reconstructs the text. Works on printed text, screenshots, signs, and even some handwriting.
