How This Works

Your skills are vectorized using TF-IDF weighting to penalize common terms and reward specific ones. Each job role is also a weighted vector. Cosine Similarity measures the angular alignment between your vector and each role's vector — magnitude-invariant, so profile size doesn't bias results.
